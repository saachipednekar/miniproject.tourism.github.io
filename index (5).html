Top Attractions

Baga Beach

Baga Beach, located in North Goa, is one of the most popular and lively beaches in the region, known for its vibrant nightlife, beach shacks, water sports, and scenic sunsets. It's a must-visit for travelers seeking the perfect mix of relaxation and adventure — from parasailing and jet skiing to dancing at iconic clubs like Tito’s and Mambo’s. The energetic vibe, delicious seafood, and stunning shoreline make Baga an unforgettable coastal experience.

Aguada Fort

Colonial-era Portuguese fort with scenic views Aguada Fort, located in North Goa, is a 17th-century Portuguese fort overlooking the Arabian Sea. It is popular for its historic significance, well-preserved structure, and breathtaking panoramic views. Every tourist visits it to explore the iconic lighthouse, ancient architecture, and to enjoy the sunset views from the ramparts. It’s a perfect blend of history, scenic beauty, and coastal charm.

Basilica of Bom Jesus

The Basilica of Bom Jesus, located in Old Goa, is one of the most iconic and revered churches in India. Built in the late 16th century, it houses the mortal remains of St. Francis Xavier, making it a significant pilgrimage site for Christians around the world. The church is a UNESCO World Heritage Site, known for its stunning Baroque architecture, detailed carvings, and spiritual atmosphere. Its historical and religious importance, along with its beautiful interior, make it a must-visit for anyone exploring Goa.
